PID and Ports
==============
A PID is a reference to a local or remote process
A Port is a reference to a resource (typically external to the application) that you will be reading ot writting to
the PID of the current process is available by calling self

---code-----
iex(1)> self
#PID<0.109.0>
---end code----

a new PID is created when you spawn a new process
